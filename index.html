ABOUT V-CUPS CAFE

Coffee beans are a great way to start off your morning. Not only do they give you a boost of caffeine, but they also provide many vitamins and minerals. These beans are rich in antioxidants, fiber, and protein. They also have a high concentration of iron, copper, zinc, manganese, and magnesium.

Coffee beans are a great resource for growers looking to increase their yields. Coffee beans are rich in caffeine, which helps stimulate the nervous system and increase alertness. Caffeine is also known to have a positive effect on the body's endocannabinoid system (ECS), which is responsible for maintaining homeostasis and regulating mood, appetite, sleep cycles, pain perception, and memory.

V-cups Cafe Shop is a coffee shop located in Tumakuru. We offer a variety of different types of coffees, teas, smoothies, juices, and snacks. Our menu changes daily, so check out our website to find out what we have going on!
